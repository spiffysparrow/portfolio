Recent Projects

Poemist

A web application for creating and sharing found poems. I built it using Ruby on Rails and React.

You are given a random passage from a public domain book and allowed to click on words or letters to create your poem out of this text.

You can view, edit, delete your poems, as well as like other peoples.

Notifications appear in the top right corner if you have unseen likes, and from there you can view the liker's profile or the poem they liked.

You can also view and edit your profile, or look at all the poems you have liked.



Zombies VS Unicorns

A javascript game for murdering unicorns.

The world has been overrun with Unicorns and it is your job as the only scrappy little zombie left to take it back.

It's a fun a silly project I created to test out JavaScript game development.
