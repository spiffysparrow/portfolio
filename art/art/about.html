About

I'm an Austin artist born and raised. All my life I've been obsessed with art; I have pages upon pages of horrible five-year-old fairy sketches to prove it.

Doodles are my lifelong love, animation my favorite medium, and canvases my reluctant frenemy.

Check out the rest of my website to see some of my varied interests, from painting, to motion graphics, to programing.
